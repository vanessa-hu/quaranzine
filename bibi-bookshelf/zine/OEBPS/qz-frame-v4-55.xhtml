THE PLIGHT OF THE MODERN CHINESE RESTAURANT

An Excerpt

(On next page)

We wanted to better understand how COVID-19 would effect “au-thentic Chinese food” places in Chinatowns versus “Americanized Chi-nese Foods” from takeout places in vastly non Asian areas to get a better sense of which type of cuisine might come out on top. These eateries, albeit different, are very special to us as sources of connections to our heritage through food, which is why we are so concerned about the det-rimental effects these restaurants are facing.

We ultimately want people to acknowledge their implicit biases as we use historical examples to prove that these anti asian sentiments can re-ally damage people’s livelihoods and eliminate their safe spaces.

by Grace Cen, Mina Lee, Jessica Wu
