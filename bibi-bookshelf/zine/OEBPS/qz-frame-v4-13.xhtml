Chen was inspired after watching a video on the “Reunion Season” in China after months of lockdown. While it was heartbreaking to think of these young children being separated from their parents, it was also beautiful to see how much joy came from simply being together again. Chen wanted to evoke that childlike, simplistic happiness in this portrayal of that reunion, and it makes her so hopeful for the day that we come out of quarantine, too!
