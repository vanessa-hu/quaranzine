Elementary School

Traffic Lights

By Karen Zheng

YELLOW - they slow down

approach me with amused smirks

RED - let’s play a game

they place their plump white

fingers on each end of their blue

eyes and pull

RED - down, Japanese

RED - sideways, Korean

RED - up, you

they laugh, clutch their bellies,

writh in delight

GREEN - I join

their game,

chant the same lies they

slap on my face

GREEN - up, that’s me!

eyes slanted upwards towards the sky

I cannot see anymore

GREEN - I stop

my face

contorts
